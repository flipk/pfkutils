in a buffer or dired open on a directory with a repo, hit 'C-x g'.

tab expands or contracts a file.
p and n move between sections.
Z stashes.
g refreshes the buffer.
s will stage changes.
on an contracted file, s will stage the file at point.
on a diff, highlight a region, s will stage the region only.
c will start commit; c again to accept command; C-c C-c will create the commit.
P can push
